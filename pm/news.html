15. June 2016

New SOLAS Regulations - Safety of Life At Sea

Verified Largely measure (VGM)

As from 1st July 2016, the International Maritime Organization (IMO) enforced the Safety of Life at Sea Conventions (SOLAS) requirements regarding the verification of the gross mass of packed containers. You will find more information as well as documents (SOLAS VGM-LCL & SOLAS VGM-FCL) under the category "information".
