Company

Sustainability, further development, quality

Portland Freight is a logistic services and consulting company focussing on project logistics. We stand for successful quality and needs-orientated fulfilment of our customer’s requirements, and support them in all project phases from the preparation and conclusion of decisions, throughout the design of effective processes and appropriate organisation, right the way up to an optimised operative implementation.

Our concept is always based on our customer’s objectives, as well as our competence and ability to react quickly, flexibly and efficiently to changes and to customer wishes.

Portland Freight is a company which is orientated towards sustainability, but in addition to economic targets, it also takes social aspects into consideration. Our employees and their expertise, motivation and assiduous nature form the basis of our success and are of central importance to us. Furthermore, we work alongside experienced engineers and experts who assist us in achieving the desired results.
