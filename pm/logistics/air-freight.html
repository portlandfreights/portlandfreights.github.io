Air freight

Today an enquiry, tomorrow a delivery

Air freight mainly concentrates on high quality and time-critical goods, and therefore our planning and processing sequences are designed for speed, reliability and flexibility when supplying decisive components for modern supply networks.

As an independent logistics service provider, we guarantee you a rapid check on freight space and the flight possibilities on the market, and take care of your shipment from A to Z, i.e. from the receipt of your request up to final delivery to the recipient. We also have many years of experience and excellent know-how in long-term and one-off projects. In summary, we provide the following services:

  • Consolidated cargo and direct service
  • Part and full charter for heavy and bulk transportation
  • Courier service
  • Project planning and execution
