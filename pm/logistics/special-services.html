Special services

Transport insurance

Despite the utmost care, transport risks can never be completely ruled out, unfortunately, the legal liability often does not cover the full potential damage. Our staff will assist you with questions about liability gaps and if necessary, arrange the relevant transport insurance accordingly.

 

Customs clearance

Whether port of Hamburg or Shanghai Airport, import, export, or transit clearance: our employees and partners can help you to negotiate your goods through any customs clearance procedure. 

 

Loadmaster

All around the construction site or cargo loading, our loadmaster are always there for you. They coordinate and monitor all processes and are your direct contact person at site. Continuous training and further education will guarantee you the newest safety standards.

 

Survey services 

The documentation of shipments can provide additional certainty by means of expert opinions. For this reason, we trust on surveyor with vast experience and expertise to ensure that all relevant details are recorded accurately and on time.

 

Pilot Car BF2 / BF3

Portland Freight offers suitable transport protection vehicles with different equipment to their customers from transport branch. If the utilization is not given by own transports, the vehicles are offered accordingly to the market.
